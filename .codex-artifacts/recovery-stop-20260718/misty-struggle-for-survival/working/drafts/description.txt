A thick, primordial fog rolls across the valleys of ancient South Africa as two rival human species collide in a brutal fight for dominance, each driven by instinct and the desperate need to claim the land as their own. Claws, spears, and raw fury meet in close-quarters violence where hesitation means death, and the mist swallows the screams of the fallen. When the haze finally lifts, only one species will remain standing—forever altering the course of human evolution on a continent that was never meant to welcome them.
