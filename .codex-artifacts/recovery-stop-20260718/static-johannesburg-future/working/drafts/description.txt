Amid the humming static of an abandoned radio tower in Johannesburg, a lonely operator picks up a signal that shouldn't exist—a voice, calm and unmistakably human, answering from a future he can barely imagine. Each fleeting exchange tightens the grip of possibility around him, forcing him to decide whether to trust a stranger across time or the silence he's always known. In a city of ten million voices, he may have finally found the one that matters most.
