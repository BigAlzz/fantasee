Beneath a sky still choked with the smoke of a forgotten war, two children kneel among the broken bones of everything they once knew — a human boy and a Neanderthal girl, strangers bound by the same silence of loss. With trembling hands, they share fire, food, and the first fragile words of a language that belongs to neither and both. In the ashes where their people tried to destroy each other, they must plant the seeds of something that has never existed before: trust.
