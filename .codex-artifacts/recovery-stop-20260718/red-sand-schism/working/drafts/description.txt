Beneath a sky bruised with ash and ancient wrath, the exiled remnants of a forgotten people march southward, their footprints staining the red sand like open wounds upon the land. In the Cape, entrenched human tribes guard their blood-claimed territories with spear and sorcery, and the coming collision of these two primordial races will carve a schism into history itself—red with war, bitter with truce, and never truly healed.
