He bears a terrible curse: every blueprint he touches reveals not just the genius of the design, but the specific ruin it will one day cause. Now, in a medieval kingdom hungry for progress, he must choose which innovations to champion and which to sabotage, knowing that each "advancement" draws the world closer to resurrecting the very machines that annihilated his own future. The whispers of cogs turning in forgotten tombs grow louder with every plan he unrolls.
