Title: Demolition Vex's Doomed Kingdom
Tone: manhwa
Style: manhwa
Concept: A special forces combat engineer wakes up in a medieval kingdom besieged by undead hordes rising from ancient cursed battlefields. His knowledge of explosives, demolition, and military engineering is the kingdom's last hope. A female necromancer turned rogue named Vex -- darkly beautiful with death-magic -- becomes his unlikely partner as they venture into cursed lands to destroy the source of the undead plague.
