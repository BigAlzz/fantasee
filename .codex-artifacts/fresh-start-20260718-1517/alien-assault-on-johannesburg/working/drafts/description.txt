The alien vessels didn’t land—they crashed into the Magaliesberg hills, cracking the earth open with a sound like breaking bones. At Hillside Stand, a handful of South African soldiers became the first to witness the war machines that unfolded from the smoke, their forms twisting the light in ways human eyes refused to understand. They held the line not to win, but to buy a few more hours for the city, their final stand already lost against an enemy that felt no mercy.
