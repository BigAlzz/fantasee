Awakened in a medieval kingdom under siege by the restless dead, Special Forces combat engineer Sergeant Reyes finds his modern knowledge of explosives is the realm's only salvation. With a satchel of detonators and claymore mines, he becomes a one-man siege-breaker, turning necromantic hordes into clouds of cursed dirt and bone. But every blast reshapes the very ground the dead rise from, promising a cataclysmic, pyrotechnic hell.
