From the shadowed servers of the NSA to the fading light of a once-great kingdom, expert hacker Anya is yanked through a rift of raw data. A sentient, consuming darkness—the corrupted Net—has woven itself into the realm’s magic, devouring ley lines like malicious code. Now, she must rewrite reality itself, wielding firewalls against shadow sorcery to reboot a world on the brink of crash.
