**Cyber Mage and the Dark Grid**

When top NSA hacker Elara Voss is violently ripped from her world and thrust into the crumbling kingdom of Aethermoor, she discovers that the "dark network" devouring its lands is horrifyingly familiar — a sentient, corrupted magical grid spreading like malware through every leyline and living soul. Armed with her modern cyber expertise and a newfound ability to *hack* the very fabric of magic, Elara must battle monstrous glitch-corrupted knights and shadowy firewall guardians to save a realm that desperately needs the one thing it never had: a firewall. But the Dark Grid is evolving fast — and it's already learned how to hack *her* back.
