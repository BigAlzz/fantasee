# Blast from the Battlefield Past

When a top-tier combat engineer is ripped from the battlefield and hurled into a crumbling medieval kingdom drowning in undead, he finds the same old war waiting for him—just with swords and sorcery instead of rifles and radios. Armed with nothing but his expertise in explosives, fortification, and tactical demolition, he'll turn castle walls into kill zones and cursed graveyards into craters. But as the ancient necromantic forces behind the horde begin to take notice of this strange outsider, the real battle—and the mystery of why he was brought here—has only just begun.
