An NSA cyber-warrior is ripped from her world of firewalls and code into a realm of swords and sorcery, only to find an ancient kingdom being devoured by a sentient, corrupted magical grid—its shadowy tendrils consuming both spells and souls. Now, her knowledge of digital viruses and network warfare may be the only weapon that can stop the Shadow Grid before it plunges the entire world into an eternal, glitching dark.
