**Explosive Catacombs**  
A seasoned combat engineer wakes amid the siege of a crumbling medieval kingdom, where the dead rise from cursed fields. Armed with modern knowledge of explosives and fortifications, he realizes his C4 and engineering principles are the only weapons that can carve a path through the supernatural hordes. Now, he must rig tombs, collapse tunnels, and turn ancient curses into catastrophic chain reactions to survive the endless night.
