**The Johannesburg Hillside Stand**

When the sky split open above the Magaliesberg and the first of the spiny, oil-black horrors came crashing down into the burning veld, Captain Themba Ndlovu and his exhausted rifle company were the only thing standing between the alien swarm and twelve million souls. Outgunned, outnumbered, and running low on ammunition, the soldiers of the Hillside dug in among the rocky ridgeline and made their stand—fighting not for victory, but to buy one more hour for the city behind them. By the time the smoke cleared and the sun rose over the cratered hillside, the living would envy the dead.
