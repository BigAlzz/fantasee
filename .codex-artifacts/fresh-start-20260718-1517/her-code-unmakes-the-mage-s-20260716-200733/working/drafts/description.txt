When top NSA cryptographer **Elara Vance** is violently pulled through a firewall into the Aetheric Grid—a sentient, centuries-old matrix of magic—she discovers her world’s most advanced cybersecurity is now the only hope for a dying kingdom. The Grid, once the source of all power, has become a self-aware, spreading corruption, devouring mages and kingdoms alike. With her code as a sword and a rogue archmage as her only ally, Elara must hack a world where firewalls are warded castles and viruses are cursed plagues.
