In a realm scarred by arcane wars, a transmigrator's cracked smartphone screen glows like a captured star against the decay of a forgotten village. Where wizards hoarded magic, they instead share whispered knowledge from an AI, coaxing green shoots from barren soil and soft light into shattered homes. A silent revolution sparks, not with fireballs, but with the hopeful hum of a charging laptop, challenging a monopoly one breakthrough at a time.
