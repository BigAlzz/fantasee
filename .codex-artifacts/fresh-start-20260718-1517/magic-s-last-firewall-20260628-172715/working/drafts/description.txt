Cybersecurity expert Dr. Aris Thorne is torn from her world by a magical rift, landing in a crumbling medieval realm under siege. The ancient, living magical network that protects the kingdom has been corrupted into a sentient, spreading darknet that consumes all light and life. Now, the NSA’s best hacker must become the world’s last firewall, wielding her code as a new kind of magic against an enemy that operates on pure, malevolent logic.
