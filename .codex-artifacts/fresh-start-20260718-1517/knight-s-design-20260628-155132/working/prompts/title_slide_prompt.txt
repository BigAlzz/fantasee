Title: Knight's Design
Tone: manhwa
Style: manhwa
Concept: A modern military engineer dies in a drone strike and wakes up as a disgraced knight in a medieval kingdom on the brink of collapse. Armed with knowledge of fortification design, ballistics, supply chain logistics, and modern military strategy, he must rebuild a broken army while navigating court intrigue. His tactical genius attracts the attention of a beautiful but deadly female spymaster named Lyra who becomes his closest ally. Together they face barbarian hordes, corrupt nobles, and a failing harvest while secretly planning a revolution in military technology.
