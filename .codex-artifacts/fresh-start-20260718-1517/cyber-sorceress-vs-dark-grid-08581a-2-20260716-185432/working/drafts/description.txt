**Cyber Sorceress vs. Dark Grid**

When top NSA cryptographer Kira Sung vanishes mid-mission, she awakens in a fractured medieval realm where ancient magic flows through a vast enchanted network—now corrupted and consuming entire kingdoms like a living virus. Armed with her hacking instincts and a mysterious grimoire that reads like corrupted code, Kira must wield firewalls and hexes alike to purge the sentient Dark Grid before it devours the last light of the realm. But the Grid whispers back—and it knows her name.
