**Ancient Rift, Modern War**

A rift tears open between worlds, unleashing the Neanderthals—ferocious, war-hardened, and wielding primal magic that bends the earth and shatters steel. As humanity's tanks crumble against towering bone constructs and shamanic flame, one soldier discovers that the key to survival lies not in technology, but in an ancient power buried in his own bloodline. The war for the future will be decided by the sins of the past.
