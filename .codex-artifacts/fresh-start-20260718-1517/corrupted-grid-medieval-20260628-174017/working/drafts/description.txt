Elena's scans of the NSA's deepest firewalls don't prepare her for the living, screaming corruption of a magical grid—an ancient power now devouring a kingdom in waves of glitching shadow. As a dark web of cracked light and silent screams consumes castles and souls, her modern cybersecurity skills become the realm's only hope. She is no longer just an analyst; she is a firewall forged in a dying world, and her terminal is now a enchanted sword.
