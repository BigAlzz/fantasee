In a realm of castles and guilds, a reluctant cartographer is cursed to see the ominous portents within every design—the plagues, collapses, and fires they will inevitably unleash. Now he must navigate the treacherous waters of medieval progress, knowing that a single brilliant invention could be the key that resurrects the very mechanical beasts that razed his own future world. His blueprints are no longer maps to prosperity, but elegantly drawn warnings of the doom they will bring.
