Selene's bones ache with the weight of 300 winters, but her purpose is ironclad: a dirge to awaken the Gate Stone will unravel her life-thread and shatter the rift for her people. As her grandson bears her toward the frozen monolith, the rift-walker Mira watches, haunted by seven visions of silence and only one of salvation. The air thins, the Stone glimmers with hungry frost, and Selene draws a final breath to sing the world open and herself closed forever.
