At dawn’s first light, a Neanderthal war party marches into a frozen forest, only to find their path blocked by the gleaming powered armor of a Concord patrol. When their leader, Korrath Stone-Hand, presses his crystal stump to the earth, the land itself answers—his body convulses and hardens into eight feet of living, gray-skinned stone. Plasma bolts strike him and flare white-hot, but he does not slow, shattering advanced weapons before standing over the soldiers to deliver a warning from the world itself.
