When a modern special forces combat engineer awakens in a crumbling medieval kingdom besieged by endless waves of undead, he finds that his expertise in explosives and demolitions is the one weapon this world has never seen. Armed with improvised grenades, battlefield ingenuity, and a soldier's ruthless pragmatism, he'll turn cursed battlefields into kill zones and shattered castles into unbreakable fortresses. But as the horde grows smarter and ancient dark forces take notice of his meddling, one question looms—can you C4 your way out of a war with death itself?
