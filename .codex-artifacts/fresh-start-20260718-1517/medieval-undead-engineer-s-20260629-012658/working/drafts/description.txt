**Medieval Undead: Engineer's Demolition**

When combat engineer Sergeant Kang Jaeha's last detonation goes sideways, he wakes surrounded by crumbling castle walls and the stench of rotting corpses clawing from cursed earth. Armed with nothing but a dead world's explosives expertise and a medieval kingdom on its last legs, he'll turn siege warfare upside down—because the undead have never faced someone who can collapse the very ground they rise from. Every crumbling wall is a kill zone, every bridge a trap, and every battlefield a demolition site waiting to be born.
