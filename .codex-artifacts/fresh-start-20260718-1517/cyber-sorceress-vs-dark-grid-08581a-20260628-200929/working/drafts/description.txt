Dragged from her high-tech reality, NSA cyber-warrior Anya Volkov finds herself in a crumbling kingdom under siege by the Dark Grid—a sentient, spreading corruption of the world's magical network. With her modern expertise in code-breaking and digital warfare, she becomes an unexpected sorceress, the only one who can debug this ancient, arcane system before it consumes all magic and life. Now, she must fight firewalls with fireballs in a battle where ones and zeros clash with runes and destiny.
