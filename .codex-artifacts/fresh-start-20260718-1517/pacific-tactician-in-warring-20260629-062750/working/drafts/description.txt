**Pacific Tactician in Warring Isles**

When a rogue wave swallows the USS *Midway's* brilliant fleet strategist whole, he awakens on the deck of a primitive warship—thrust into a fractured archipelago where dozens of island kingdoms bleed each other dry in endless naval warfare. Armed with centuries of advanced maritime knowledge and a warrior's grit, he'll forge alliances with fierce island princesses, revolutionize ship design, and unleash tactics these warring isles have never dreamed of—while ancient forces lurking beneath the deep stir at his arrival.
