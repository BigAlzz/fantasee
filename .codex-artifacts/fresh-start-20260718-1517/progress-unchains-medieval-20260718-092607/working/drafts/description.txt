A cursed sight condemns Alaric to see the ruin wrought by every invention he sketches, from crumbling fortresses to plagues born of alchemy. Haunted by this deadly foresight, he must tread carefully through a kingdom hungry for progress, for each blueprint he creates risks awakening the rotting clockwork horrors from his own decimated future. His greatest designs are blueprints for doom, and the past is learning to scream in the language of gears.
