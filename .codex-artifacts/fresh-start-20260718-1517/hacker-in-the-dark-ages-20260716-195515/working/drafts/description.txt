When NSA's top cybersecurity analyst, Anya, is torn from her world and into a crumbling medieval kingdom, she finds the real threat isn't a siege engine, but a living, parasitic magical grid—a dark network devouring reality itself. Armed with logic and code against a malicious consciousness that wields curses like malware, she must hack with sword and spell to save a world that doesn't comprehend the enemy already inside its walls.
