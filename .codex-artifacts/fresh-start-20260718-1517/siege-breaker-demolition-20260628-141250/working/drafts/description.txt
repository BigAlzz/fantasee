Trapped in a crumbling medieval kingdom where the dead claw their way back from cursed earth, a modern special forces combat engineer must turn barrels of black powder, siege blueprints, and improvised explosives into humanity's last line of defense against an endless undead horde. With every blast that shakes the ancient walls, he carves a legend the kingdom never knew it needed—but the cursed battlefields hold secrets even C4 can't breach.
