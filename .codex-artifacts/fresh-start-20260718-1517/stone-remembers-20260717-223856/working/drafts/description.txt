In the frozen dawn, a Stone-Born war party confronts a Concord patrol of armored soldiers. As plasma fire glances harmlessly off Korrath Stone-Hand’s living stone hide, he moves through their onslaught and methodically shatters their advanced weapons with his crystal fist. Standing over them, a monolith of ancient power, he delivers the earth's judgment in a voice of grinding stone: *Go home. Tell them the earth remembers.*
