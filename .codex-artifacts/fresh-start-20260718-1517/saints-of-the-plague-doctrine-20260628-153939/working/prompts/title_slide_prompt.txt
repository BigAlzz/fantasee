Title: Saints of the Plague Doctrine
Tone: manhwa
Style: manhwa
Concept: A pharmaceutical researcher is reborn in a plague-ridden fantasy world where disease wipes out entire cities. Her modern knowledge of microbiology, epidemiology, and vaccine development gives her an edge no one else has. A female healer-priestess named Amara becomes her devoted companion as they race to develop cures while battling a fanatical religious order that sees disease as divine punishment.
