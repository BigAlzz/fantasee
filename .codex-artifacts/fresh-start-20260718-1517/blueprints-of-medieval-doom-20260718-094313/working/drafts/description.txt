When a disgraced alchemist brushes a blueprint, he doesn't see a design—he sees the blood, smoke, and ruin it is destined to create. Now a fugitive in a medieval kingdom poised between stagnation and catastrophe, he must choose which innovations to permit, knowing each calculation could resurrect the very apocalypse that erased his own future. His touch doesn't just draw lines; it etches fate, and every page turned is a step closer to unleashing the mechanical doom that awaits.
