When Sergeant Jae, a ruthlessly efficient combat engineer, regains consciousness in a crumbling medieval watchtower, the air screams with the shrieks of the risen dead. His enemies are no longer insurgents, but skeletal legions boiling from cursed soil—yet his knowledge of explosives, kill zones, and structural demolition becomes the most terrifying magic this kingdom has ever seen. He turns ancient stone and rotten wood into a symphony of shrapnel, ready to teach the undead the true meaning of controlled demolition.
