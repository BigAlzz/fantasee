Grenade in hand and C4 in his pack, Sergeant First Class Marcus Cole awakens in a crumbling fortress besieged by shambling horrors. His modern combat engineering isn't just a skill here—it's a sorcery of shattered stone and controlled detonations, a terrifying new magic the undead legions have never faced. This is the alchemy of destruction, rewritten for a world of swords and sorcery, where one man's expertise will either save a kingdom or bring it down in an explosive spectacle.
