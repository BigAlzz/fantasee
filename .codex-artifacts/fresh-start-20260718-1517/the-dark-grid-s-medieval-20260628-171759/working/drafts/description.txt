When elite NSA cryptographer Dr. Seo Yuna traces a sentient malware breach, she's violently pulled through the digital veil into a crumbling medieval kingdom powered by a luminous magical grid — now corrupted by the very dark network she was hunting. Armed with nothing but her hacking instincts and a mysterious sigil burned into her wrist, she must forge alliances with sword-wielding knights and battle-mages to wage a war she was never supposed to survive. As the Grid spreads like a living plague, devouring kingdoms and twisting souls, Yuna realizes the code haunting her world and the ancient magic devouring *this* one share a terrifying, ancient origin.
