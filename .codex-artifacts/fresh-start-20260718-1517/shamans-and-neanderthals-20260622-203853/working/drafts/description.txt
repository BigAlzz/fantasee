Beneath a sky torn by primordial magic, the scent of fear is sharper than any spearpoint. Two ancient lines of mystics—the human shamans with their spirit-song and the Neanderthals with their bone-and-earth sorcery—must forge a truce against the whispered thing rising from the world's wound. Yet every shared ritual is a battlefield, and the very power that could save them may be the first to drive them to war.
