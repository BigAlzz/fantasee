Title: Catalog, Kin, Cedar
Tone: dramatic
Style: cinematic realism
Concept: Private Elena Vasquez, 19, Concord infantrywoman, tracks Neanderthals through old-growth forest. Her HUD labels them as catalog numbers KN-0782 through KN-0787. At dawn they find the Neanderthals gathered around an ancient cedar. An old woman has her hand on the bark. She is weeping. Elena's HUD says THREAT ASSESSMENT: LOW. Her sergeant orders her to fire. She does not fire. The old woman looks at her. Elena's HUD glitches. For one second she sees a face instead of a number. Scarred. Tired. Silver eyes. The sound of someone coming home.
