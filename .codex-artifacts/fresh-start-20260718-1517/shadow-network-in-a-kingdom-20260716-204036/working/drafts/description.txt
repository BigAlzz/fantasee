A brilliant NSA analyst, Lin, is summoned not by a server crash, but by a rift in reality itself. Now in a crumbling kingdom where magic flows like data, she must use her code-breaking skills to fight a sentient, corrupted magical network—a dark web of glitches and despair consuming the realm's very lifeforce. Her quantum laptop might be the only key to debugging a kingdom's dying weave before it's silenced forever.
